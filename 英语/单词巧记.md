> ***indication***
> 名词：表示，暗示，迹象，指示
> ```
> 1.表明某事的迹象
> an indication of + 某事
> give/provide an indication that + 从句
>
> 2.建议或指示
> indication for + 某种用途或治疗
> give clear indications
>
> 3.信号或标志
> as an indication of + 某种信号
> ```
